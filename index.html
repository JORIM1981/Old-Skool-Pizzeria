about

OLD SKOOL PIZZERIA! is a place for simplicity. Good food, good beer, and good service. Simple is the name of the game, and we’re good at finding it in all the right places, even in your dining experience. We’re a small group from Kitengela, Athi River who make simple food possible. Come join us and see what simplicity tastes like.
